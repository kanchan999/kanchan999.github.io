INTRODUCTION

Bridging rigorous theory with practical impact in optimization and machine learning.

I am a researcher and faculty member whose work centers on evolutionary computation and metaheuristics, with a particular focus on understanding and mitigating structural bias in population-based algorithms. My research combines theoretical analysis with the development of practical frameworks that improve optimization performance across diverse problem domains.

I am excited to contribute to the revival of Nalanda University as a global center of learning while pursuing research in optimization, transfer learning, and their applications in emerging areas such as dynamic graphs and non-stationary environments.
